Connect the Pipes

Left click to rotate pipes clockwise, shift then left-click to rotate anticlockwise.
Right click to mark a pipe as confirmed.
Ctrl+Z to Undo, Ctrl+Y to Redo.
How to play

Rules:
 1. All pipes must be connected to the source of water at the centre.
2. There should not be any leakages within the pipes.
3. Pipes should not form any cycles.
If a cycle is formed, the involved nodes will be highlighted (As well as some unrelated nodes in some cases)
4. There may exist multiple solutions to the game.
